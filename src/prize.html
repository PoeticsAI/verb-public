Rules & Regs

Deadline for submission is midnight on the 1st August, 2022. Submission will be via the app - look out for the prize submission button in the coming weeks.

All entrants will receive a permanent discount code for use of Verb indefinitely

You retain all rights to your work.

The judges will be the three Verb founders, Ryan, Ben and Jeff. All entries will be read in full by all judges. The critic score from Verb will inform our decision.

Your piece should be between 5,000 and 7,500 words.

You must use Verb to write at least half your entry

You must use the plan function to scaffold out your story
